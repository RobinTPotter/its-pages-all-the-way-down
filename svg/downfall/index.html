Cardboard Downfall

Printable templates for coardboard version of Downfall. This is size to fit a 16mm diameter marble.

Print and construct pages 1-7 twice, can either print onto cerial box (should fit Aldi Hoops) or onto paper and temporarily glue to card to enable accurate cutting.

page 8 are shafts which join the wheel pairs. Construct the shafts and use as a guide to make holes in a card base, use at least 2 in hold the weight of the wheels and marbles.
